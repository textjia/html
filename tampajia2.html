  19. YBOR CITY - Ybor City is a historic neighborhood located just northeast of downtown Tampa, Florida. It was founded in the 1880s by cigar manufacturer Vicente Martinez-Ybor, who was drawn to the area by the nearby port and the large population of Cuban and Spanish immigrants who were skilled in cigar-making. The neighborhood quickly grew into a bustling community of cigar factories, worker housing, and a vibrant cultural scene.

    During the late 19th and early 20th centuries, Ybor City was known as the "Cigar Capital of the World" and was home to more than 200 cigar factories. The neighborhood was also home to a diverse community of immigrants from Cuba, Spain, Italy, and other countries, who brought their own cultures, traditions, and cuisines to the area.

    In the 1920s and 1930s, Ybor City went through a period of decline as the cigar industry shifted to other parts of the country and the Great Depression took its toll on the community. However, the neighborhood managed to survive and in the 1980s and 1990s, Ybor City began to experience a resurgence in popularity as an entertainment district with nightclubs, restaurants, and shops. Today, Ybor City is considered one of Tampa's most popular tourist destinations, known for its historic architecture, cultural heritage, and vibrant nightlife.

    Ybor City has been designated as a National Historic Landmark District and the neighborhood's architecture, such as the brick buildings and ornate wrought-iron balconies, are well preserved. Many of the buildings have been repurposed, such as the cigar factories turned into offices and shops, preserving the neighborhood's unique history.

    2. Park your car - There is a small parking lot at the intersection where you can park your car. Alternatively, you can park on the side of the road.
    3. Walk to the starting point - From the parking lot, walk to the starting point, which is marked by a small sign.
    4. Read the sign - The sign at the starting point explains the history and legend of Spook Hill, as well as the steps for experiencing the optical illusion.
    5. Place your car in neutral - According to the legend, when you place your car in neutral and let go of the brake, it will appear to roll uphill.
    6. Observe the illusion - As you release the brake, your car should appear to roll uphill. The effect is caused by the angle of the road and the surrounding landscape, and is not actually gravity-defying.
    7. Take pictures and enjoy the view - After observing the optical illusion, you can take pictures and enjoy the surrounding scenery. Spook Hill is located in a beautiful area with stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
